This tool was inspired by a blog post by @david_a_eaves and created during some late night hacking at the Super Happy Hacker House #1 by @lukec and @kevinj.

Recollect is a service, but also a case study designed to show city councils the services, resources and ingenuity that can emerge when cities open up and share their data.

If you have questions regarding Recollect, please consult Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).
